POST-SESSION TASKS

Session Recommendations

Preferred: Provided recommendations on suggestion sheet (in-person at end of session)
Timeline: In the moment | Reminder: Take a photo of suggestion sheet for attachment to SOAP note
Alternative: Send email of recommendations to family
Timeline: 2-3 working days (for family's quick use before next team member's session) | NOTE: Aim to provide recommendations on suggestion sheet in-person if possible

ESDM Checklist & Supplementary Handout

Update the ESDM checklist - observations and notes
Timeline: Same day or next day | NOTE: If in Eastern, Western, or Northern Zone, the digital copy of the checklist needs to be updated
Complete the OT/SLP Supplementary handout
Timeline: Same day or next day

Update ShareVision

Complete attendance tracker
Timeline: Before next team member's session | Best to do along with SOAP note
Complete SOAP Note
Timeline: Within 1 week (before next team member session) | NOTE: Upload the OT/SLP Supplementary handout as an attachment

Referrals / Phone or Email Contacts

Complete required actions on any recommended referrals
Timeline: 1-2 weeks | NOTE: This includes updating the "Community Partners and Programs" tab in SV

Mileage

Update travel expense form (if travel with claimable mileage)
Timeline: To claim travel expenses, the form minimally needs to be completed & submitted to supervisor & finance monthly - by the 3rd business day of the following month
